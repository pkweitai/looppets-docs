LoopPets — Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 7, 2025

Summary

  • LoopPets offers general wellness support and is not a medical device.
  • We access Apple Health data only if you grant permission, to create clear, educational insights.
  • We do not sell your data. You can revoke Health access anytime in the Apple Health app.

Information We Collect

  • You provide: mood check-ins, journal entries, optional voice input, and (if you create an account) basic account info.
  • Apple Health (HealthKit): heart rate, steps, sleep states, active energy, mindfulness minutes, respiratory rate, blood oxygen, body temperature — if you allow.
  • Device & usage: app-generated device ID, diagnostics, crash logs.
  • Media & integrations: microphone/speech recognition when used; YouTube playback may send usage data to YouTube/Google.

How We Use Information

  • Provide features (check-ins, insights, reminders, virtual pet responses)
  • Personalize content and educational explanations
  • Improve reliability and security

Data Storage & Sharing

  • On device: your logs and journals are stored locally.
  • On our server (if you opt in): device ID and limited metadata; no Apple Health data is stored on our servers.
  • Third-party processors: we use infrastructure/analytics/AI providers bound by confidentiality and security obligations. No advertisers.

Your Choices & Controls

  • Manage Apple Health access in Health → Sharing → Apps → LoopPets.
  • Use text instead of voice input, and control notifications in iOS Settings.
  • Request data deletion or export via the app or by email.

Data Retention & Security

We retain data as long as needed to provide the service. We use technical and organizational measures to protect information, though no method is 100% secure.
