Quick Troubleshooting

  • No notifications? iOS Settings → Notifications → LoopPets → Allow Notifications.
  • Missing Apple Health data? Health → Sharing → Apps → LoopPets → enable read permissions (heart rate, steps, sleep, etc.).
  • Voice input not working? Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone / Speech Recognition → enable for LoopPets.
  • Audio/lofi silent? Check Silent Mode switch, volume, then force quit and relaunch the app.
  • Icon/screens not updated? Delete previous TestFlight build and reinstall; iOS may cache assets.

FAQs

How do I enable Apple Health access?

Open the Apple Health app → Sharing → Apps → LoopPets and turn on read permissions for the metrics you want to see: heart rate, steps, sleep states, active energy, mindfulness minutes, respiratory rate, blood oxygen, body temperature.

How do I delete my data or account?

In the app: Settings → Delete Account & Data. Or email support@looppets.app from the address on your account with the subject “Data Deletion”.

Is LoopPets a medical device?

No. LoopPets offers general wellness support and is not a medical device or a substitute for professional care.

How do I control notifications?

iOS Settings → Notifications → LoopPets. You can enable/disable and choose styles. Inside the app, you can also turn off gentle check‑in reminders.

What if I don’t want to use voice input?

Voice is optional. You can type your check‑ins and journal entries anytime.
